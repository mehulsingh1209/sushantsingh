About Sushant Medico

Your Trusted Healthcare Partner in Birgunj Since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Sushant Singh Rajput, Sushant Medico began as a small neighborhood pharmacy with a big vision - to provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare to the people of Birgunj.

What started as a modest store has grown into one of Birgunj's most trusted pharmacies, serving thousands of satisfied customers each year. Our commitment to genuine products, expert advice, and compassionate service has been the cornerstone of our success.

Our Mission

To provide quality healthcare products with professional advice, ensuring our customers receive the best care and value for their money.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We sell only genuine, quality products
  • Expertise: Our trained pharmacists provide reliable advice
  • Compassion: We treat every customer like family
  • Community: We're committed to Birgunj's health and wellness
